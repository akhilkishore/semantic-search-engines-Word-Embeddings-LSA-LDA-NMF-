  Flanked by the Western Ghats and the Arabian Sea, the Malabar area is responsible for having provided some of the most delicious and flavourful additions to Kerala cuisine. It has a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian fare and people flock to these parts to taste the unique flavour of the food here. Here is a collection of recipes of some of the premier dishes that best represent Malabar’s food culture. The very aroma of Malabar dishes will surely transport you to the friendly shores from which this delicious cuisine originated.
