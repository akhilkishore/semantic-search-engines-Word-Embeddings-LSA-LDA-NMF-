  Our visitors have usually a general idea of the specific places inside the State they wish to visit. From centres of art, locations with rare flora,Ayurveda centres and treatment hospitals to locations with a breath-taking view, destinations in God’s Own Country all vary in what they can offer our guests. Thus, the list of places to visit may seem exhaustive considering the sheer range on display. To help with the planning and make sifting through the information regarding our various traditions and customs less cumbersome, we have compiled a list of places of interest that have been chosen by a majority of our visitors. These contain places that ought to give you a good starting point while planning your trip to Kerala. The images and pictures in high resolution shall convey what a thousand words will not be able to. We welcome you to browse through them and get a basic idea about what awaits you in God's Own Country.
