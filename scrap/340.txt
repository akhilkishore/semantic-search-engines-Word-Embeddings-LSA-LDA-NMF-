   The Bamboo Grove – Eco-lodge & Centre for experiential learning Forest department of Kerala, took initiate to construct eco lodge for experimental learning to support local communities in Thekkedy. The 15 bamboo cottages constructed with hygienic and modern furnishings located very close to Thekkedy Wild life. The ambiance of Bamboo Grove provide very good view to the forest, listen to the whistling of bamboo culms, chirping of birds and the flow of small rivulets emerging out from the swamps of the surrounding Periyar forest The Bamboo Grove programme also offers an eco-friendly environment to conduct seminars, symposiums, workshops and training programs in the centrally located ‘Kalari’, a conference hall with audio-visual facilities. The Bamboo Grove package consists of an orientation programme, a visit to a tribal colony, wildlife film shows, bird watching, trekking and a complimentary boat ride on the famous Periyar Lake. Lunch and dinner on the first day and breakfast on the second day are included in the package. Cost not included or Additional Cost – Entrance Fee.
