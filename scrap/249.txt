Day 5Khajuraho-Jhansi-Agra After breakfast drive Khajuraho to Jhansi to take your train for Agra. On arrival at Agra Railway Station you will be transferred to Hotel. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 6 Agra- Fatehpur Sikri  Jaipur Morning after breakfast enjoy the sightseeing tour of Agra, visiting Taj Mahal The inimitable poem in white marble is the finest expression of love of an emperor for his queen situated on the banks of the river Yamuna. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan for his queen Mumtaz Mahal, in 1632.Agra Fort: Built by the great Emperor Akbar in 1565 A.D., the fort is a masterpiece of design and construction. Afternoon drive to Jaipur, en route visit Fatehpur Sikri – The imperial city of the Mughal dynasty between 1571 and 1584, Fatehpur Sikri was built by Mughal Emperor Akbar. After visit continue drive to Jaipur and check into hotel. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 7Jaipur Breakfast at hotel, thereafter visit Amber Fort – The Old Capital of Rulers of Jaipur, here one takes an Elephant to ride to the palace situated on the hill. Afternoon visit city including City Palace, Jantar-Mantar, Hawa Mahal, Birla Mandir. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 8.Jaipur Pushkar Breakfast at hotel, then after drive to Pushkar and check-in to hotel. Afternoon visit the Lord Brahma’s temple and The Holy Lake of Pushkar. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 9Puskar  Jodhpur Breakfast at hotel, then after drive to Jodhpur and check-in to hotel. Afternoon enjoy city tour of Jodhpur visiting ? Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Evening walk in bustling bazaars of Jodhpur. Overnight stay at the Resort/Hotel.     Day 10Jodhpur-Mount Abu Breakfast at hotel, then after drive to Mount Abu and check into hotel. Balance of the day is free for your own activities. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 11Mount Abu Morning after breakfast enjoy the sightseeing tour of Mount Abu, visiting Adhar Devi Temple and Guru Shikhar, Dilwara Jain Temple, also visit The Naki Lake and Sun Set Point. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 12Mount Abu  Rankapur Breakfast at hotel, then after drive to Udaipur, en route visit Jain Temple of Ranakpur. After visit continue drive to Udaipur and check into hotel. Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 13Udaipur Breakfast at hotel, then after sightseeing tour of Udaipur includes the visits of City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Bharatiya Lok Kala Museum, Maharana Pratap and Sahelion Ki Bari, Overnight stay at the Hotel.     Day 14Departure After breakfast you are transferred to airport for your flight for onwards journey.
