                            Cochin - Munnar -  Kumarakom - Cochin                         Are you looking for Kerala tour itinerary covering the best family friendly destinations? Plan an inspiring Kerala tour covering Cochin Munnar Kumarakom Cochin with family and enjoy the exotic charm of God's Own Country. 1 
                                                                        Arrival at Kochi airport and transfer to Munnar.  On the way enjoy some waterfalls and natural scenic spots. On arrival at Munnar check into Hotel and relax for the rest of the day. Overnight stay at the hotel will be arranged.                                                                        
 2 
                                                                        After breakfast spend the day with local sightseeing. Situated at the confluence of three mountain streams - Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni and Kundale, Munnar is 1600 Meter above sea level. This majestic hill station was once the summer resort of the British government in South India. Local sightseeing of Munnar includes visit to Mattupetty Dam, Kundale Lake and Echo point. Overnight stay is at Munnar.                                                                        
 3 
                                                                        After breakfast proceed to Rajamalai were the rarest species of mountain goat, "Nilgiri Thars", can be spotted. Top station is one of another tourist interesting place. Relax rest of the day. Overnight is at hotel in Munnar                                                                        
 4 
                                                                        After breakfast check out from Hotel and drive to Kumarakom (85 km - 2 Hours drive), check in to the hotel on arrival. Spend the afternoon at leisure and overnight stay is arranged in Kumarakom.                                                                        
 5 
                                                                        After breakfast check out from the Hotel and visit Kumarakom - a tiny backwater hamlet perched on the western banks of the Vembanadu Lake. The stretch of backwater is spread over 900 sq. km. Choose a canoe, motor boat or a houseboat and devour the beauty of this backwater paradise at its best. Overnight stay is arranged in Kumarakom.                                                                        
 6 
                                                                        After breakfast check out from the Hotel and drive to Cochin Airport / railway station (depend on your departure place) for your flight / train back home, feeling contented about a wonderful holiday you spent in the God's Own Country.
